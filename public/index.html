About Me

Hi, I'm the builder 👋

I'm a college student who spent way too long staring at my degree audit trying to figure out what Gen Eds I still needed. I kept opening the same multi-page PDF, cross-referencing course catalogs, and scrolling through Reddit hoping someone had already figured it out.

So I built ClearPath — a tool that does all of that automatically. Upload your degree audit, and AI instantly tells you what's done, what's left, and which courses students actually recommend.

My goal: no student should waste time decoding their own academic requirements when that time could be spent actually learning.

The Process

How ClearPath works.

Three simple steps, powered by AI, built for students who just want a straight answer.

1

Download & Upload Your Degree Audit

Log into your university's student portal (LionPath for Penn State), navigate to your degree audit, and download it as a PDF. Then drop it into ClearPath.

LionPath compatibleAny university PDFDrag & drop
2

AI Reads & Parses Every Requirement

Claude AI reads your entire degree audit — every category, every credit count, every course completed. It simultaneously searches Reddit, RateMyProfessors, and student forums for courses students actually recommend for each Gen Ed category you still need.

Claude AI (Anthropic)Live web searchReddit + RMP data~20–30 seconds
3

Get Your Personalized Plan

You see a clean dashboard with every Gen Ed category color-coded by status, credits remaining, and specific course recommendations tagged "Easy," "Popular," or "Useful for your major." Click any card to expand and see real student-sourced course descriptions.
